I'm Aliza, a 23-year-old Pakistani Multidisciplinary Designer, who loves collaboration, curiosity, and complexity. I have an MFA in Design and Technology from Parsons School of Design and a Bachelor of Industrial Design from NUST, Pakistan.

I care about the relationships and politics of spaces, human senses, technologies, and scale, and I want to be creating the kind of design work being done at HUSH. I'm interested in tactile technology and experiential design that evokes something – a memory, a sense of place, an emotion, a transcendence, a texture, a thought, etc.

You can view my skills and experience on my resume, but what I really care about is working on meaningful concepts through making, experimentation, collaboration and drinking tea. Then, using whatever skills, tools, materials, or mediums necessary to beautifully craft a concept into existance.

I’d love to contribute my skills, ideas, and perspectives at HUSH while learning and growing as a designer. What I bring with me is a perpetual curiosity, an unconventional multi-cultural educational background, my unique perspective, a love for work driven by strong concepts, hands that love to tinker and make, and a resilient spirit.
